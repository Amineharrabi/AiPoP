# About AiPoP

## üåü Vision

AiPoP (AI Progress or Propaganda) was born from the need to bring objective analysis to the rapidly evolving AI landscape. In a world where AI developments make headlines daily, distinguishing genuine technological progress from market hype has become increasingly crucial for investors, researchers, and decision-makers.

## üéØ Mission

Our mission is to provide a data-driven framework for measuring and analyzing the relationship between AI hype and concrete progress. By combining multiple data sources and sophisticated analytics, we aim to:

1. Create transparency in AI industry dynamics
2. Enable informed decision-making
3. Identify potential market bubbles early
4. Track genuine technological advancement
5. Foster responsible AI development and adoption

## üîç Methodology

### Data Integration

AiPoP takes a comprehensive approach by analyzing:

- **Market Metrics**: Stock prices, trading volumes, and financial indicators
- **Social Signals**: Reddit discussions, news coverage, and social media sentiment
- **Technical Progress**: GitHub activity, research papers, and model adoption rates

### Analysis Framework

1. **Hype Measurement**

   - Sentiment analysis of news and social media
   - Volume and intensity of AI-related discussions
   - Market enthusiasm indicators

2. **Reality Assessment**

   - Concrete performance metrics
   - Adoption rates and real-world implementation
   - Technical advancement indicators

3. **Bubble Detection**
   - Gap analysis between hype and reality
   - Pattern recognition in historical bubbles
   - Early warning system for market distortions

## üí° Innovation

AiPoP brings several innovative elements to AI industry analysis:

1. **Multi-dimensional Analysis**: Integration of market, social, and technical metrics
2. **Quantitative Framework**: Data-driven approach to measuring AI progress
3. **Real-time Monitoring**: Continuous tracking and alert system
4. **Historical Context**: Pattern matching with previous technology bubbles
5. **Open Architecture**: Extensible design for adding new data sources and metrics
